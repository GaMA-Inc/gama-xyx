Gaze and Movement Analysis

A spin-off company from the University of Alberta, GaMA’s goal is to tap into the wealth of human performance data all around us – translating insight from academia to real-world settings.

We have completed projects across domains, including fitness to drive assessment and AR/VR training, and are currently working with ZerOne on their launch of a cutting-edge sports training facility in Edmonton, AB.
